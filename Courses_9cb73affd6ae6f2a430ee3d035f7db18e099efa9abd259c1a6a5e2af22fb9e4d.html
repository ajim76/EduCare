Foundation Courses

To get the basic fundamental knowledge before getting into first year.

 Let's Start

Introduction to AI

The purpose of this course is to provide a broad background on what AI is all about, its implications, and how to manage it to derive the best out of this emerging technology. It explores problem solving by searching, machine learning, robotics and using python programming framework.

Introduction to Electronics Engineering

This course introduces the characteristics and applications of electronics devices and circuits. Emphasis is placed on selection of components, biasing and building applications.

Introduction to Civil Engineering

The main objective of the course is to focus on building components, building planning principles, property transaction procedures, surveying and different modes of transportation.

Introduction to Thermodynamics

Aim of this course is to provide students the basic concepts of thermodynamics systems and their  applications.It also covers the basic properties of gases, liquid and vapors, energy, entropy, laws of thermodynamics of energy conversion systems.

Introduction to Engineering Materials

This course will familiarize the student with the properties of engineering materials and composite materials.The student will be  introduced to the evaluation of mechanical properties by various methods.

Introduction to Mechanical Engineering

This course consists of basic knowledge in mechanical and automobile engineering, which is of key importance to the students of those engineering streams.The units in the course covers all the fundamentals of mechanical and automobile engineering.
